Harmony Checker

Ensuring Harmonious Communication

A tool to detect and analyze hate speech and unharmonious language. Powered by GPT.

About

Harmony Checker is a collaborative initiative by Youth Development Foundation, Department of Human Rights and Minority Affairs, Goverment of Punjab, technology partner Code For Pakistan supported by NED, dedicated to promoting harmonious communication online. Our tool utilizes advanced natural language processing to identify and analyze unharmonious language and hate speech. By fostering a safer and more inclusive online environment, we aim to contribute to societal well-being. This collaboration reflects our commitment to building bridges and fostering understanding among communities.

Features

Text Analysis

Instantly analyze text submissions for unharmonious language or hate speech. Our sophisticated algorithms understand context and cultural nuances, ensuring accurate and meaningful analysis.

PDF Analysis

Upload PDF documents for in-depth analysis. Harmony Checker meticulously scans the content, identifying and categorizing potential instances of hate speech or unharmonious language.

Web URL Analysis

Analyze content directly from web pages by submitting URLs. Our tool evaluates the web content in real-time, providing a comprehensive report on any detected unharmonious language or hate speech.

How It Works

Submit Content

Users provide text, PDFs, or URLs for analysis.

Analysis

Harmony Checker assesses content for potential hate speech.

Report Generation

The system generates detailed reports, categorizing issues.

Actionable Insights

Users receive recommendations to address identified issues.
